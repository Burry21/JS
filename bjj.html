Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

Our Brazilian Jiu Jitsu teaching is world class. BJJ can be used in many situations in a person's daily life, from something serious such as self-defence, to a competition with your peers. We teach BJJ in a clean, modern facility with appropriate safety measures in place. All our classes are beginner-friendly and the coaches are informed how on how to teach everyone effectively. We cover all the basic submissions, positions, escapes for beginners - and for the advanced students, we also facilitate leg locks and more advanced grappling - in addition to our fantastic wrestling class! We are open 7 days a week, feel free to visit us!
