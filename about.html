Team

Predators Combat Club was established in 2021. We comprise of over a hundred members, many regulars - and often form different walks of life. We have an exciting learning environment, DBS qualififed coaches who are active competitors themselves. Our students benefit as they know they are getting knowledge from the best in the UK. Students build their confidence by realising them how much more they can do. Everyone is also welcoming towards one-another and beginners are always supported.
