Why do Brazilian Jiu Jitsu?

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is a full body martial art. Although it has traditional roots, at Predators, we practice BJJ in a relaxed, casual environment with people from all walks of life. From full-time working fathers, students and new mothers - everyone gets to train and enjoy their experience. This is just a portion of the benefits on offer. Give us a visit!

The Club

The club was estalished in 2021, under head coach, Firas Zahabi. He received his Black belt in 2020, under Mario Sukata of the Predators Global Association. Firas has taught around the world since acquiring his Brown belt and has multiple IBJJF accolades. The club has a relaxed environment, modern facility with spring-loaded mats, male and female changing rooms - and an AC system for the hot days! We always welcome beginners and visitors.
